    SMG2: More Missions is planned to be a hack of the entire game.


    It will have 4 demos total:

    Demo 1

    1 world of levels.

    Demo 2

    3 worlds of levels.

    Demo 3

    4-5 worlds of levels.

    Release

    The full game.


    Demo 1 is planned to be released by the end of June in 2021.


    Notice: This hack may be difficult for certain people. An easy mode will be released eventually.
