How to install


Riivolution

Installing is very simple if you already have riivolution.

Simply drag the "SMG2MM" and "riivolution" folders onto the root (parent directory) of your sd card.

Then, open riivolution and enable my hack. Make sure no other patches are enabled. Enjoy!


Dolphin

Since Dolphin has recently added support for riivolution patches, make sure you're on the latest BETA/DEVELOPER version.

Right click on your ISO/WBFS, click "Start with Riivolution patches..." and select the xml.

If you want to move the hack, move the "riivolution" and "SMG2MM" folders to be in the same place.

You cannot have ANY cheats enabled as these will crash the game.

Finally, enable the hack. Then, click start! Enjoy.
